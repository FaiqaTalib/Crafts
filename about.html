About Us

Our Story Since 1972

Venus Handicrafts was founded in 1972 with a simple passion for traditional Indian craftsmanship. Over 50 years, we've grown from a small workshop to a leading manufacturer and exporter of decorative handicrafts reaching customers worldwide.

1972 - The Beginning

Started with iron handicrafts in a small family workshop.

1990s - Global Expansion

Began exporting to Europe and USA, adding glass and brass lines.

Today - Certified Excellence

Certified manufacturer serving 50+ countries with diverse range.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To craft high-quality handicrafts that preserve traditional artistry while meeting modern global standards. Timely delivery and customer satisfaction drive everything we do.

Vision

To be the preferred partner for handicraft importers worldwide, known for innovation, reliability and beauty in every piece.

Quality Standards & Global Presence

We adhere to international quality standards, using premium materials and skilled artisans. Our products undergo rigorous quality checks before export.

Global Markets: Europe, USA, Middle East, Australia, Asia. We ship worldwide with reliable logistics partners.

Quality Certifications

Fully certified manufacturer with focus on sustainable practices.

Export Services

Bulk orders, custom designs, private labeling available.

Achievements

50+ years experience, thousands satisfied customers, repeat business from top importers.
